# Tension: innovation-vs-stability

> The conflict between introducing new capabilities, approaches, or technologies and preserving the predictability of an existing system.

---

## Competing concerns

- exploring new capabilities, technologies, or approaches
- preserving predictable behavior for existing users and dependents

---

## Common contexts where it emerges

- introducing new technologies into a production stack
- adopting architectural patterns that break prior conventions
- expanding the surface of a system used by external consumers
- experimental features in shared infrastructure

---

## Typical tradeoffs

- favoring innovation: capability gains, learning, differentiation, unknown failure modes, higher operational risk
- favoring stability: operational predictability, lower incident risk, slower adoption of better options, risk of systemic stagnation
