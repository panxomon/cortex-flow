# Primitive: accept

## Meaning

The artifact is contextually acceptable under the active policy, given the tensions identified and the rationale produced. No further mediation is required at this time.

## What it is

- a contextual position of acceptance for the current moment
- a traceable record that mediation occurred and produced no outstanding concerns

## What it is not

- a declaration of correctness
- a universal validation
- a permanent state
- a release of the artifact from future revisiting

## Required fields

- originating tension(s)
- contextual rationale
- active policy reference
- revisability conditions (even acceptance is revisable when context changes)

## Drift risk

Without explicit revisability conditions, `accept` can silently become equivalent to `ignored afterwards`. The revisability condition is mandatory.
